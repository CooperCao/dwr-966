D-Link DWR-966
Advanced
SMS

The short message function enables users to send and read messages through the DWR-966.
  » New SMS: Used for sending an SMS to a receiver; enter the phone number of the receiver.
  » Inbox: Read SMSs in the Inbox.
  » Outbox: The SMSs that you have sent.
  » Draftbox: The draft SMSs that have not been sent.
  » SMS on SIM Card: The SMSs saved in the SIM card.
  » SIM Setting: Specify the SMS center number here for the SMS service.
